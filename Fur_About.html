What is a Furry?

In general terms, a furry is somebody who is an enthusiast for animal characters with human characteristics. Or they may just enjoy the art style of a fursona or an anthro character

What is an Anthro Character?

Anthropomorphic characters, Antro for short, are animalistic characters which have features of humans. E.g. An anthropomorphic animal may walk on its hind legs and have a human like figure

Why do People like Furries and Anthro Character?

Some people may just prefer being a furry rather than just a usual person and some people may just decide they enjoy the art style. It's up to the person really. They may decide they just want to be a furry cause it looks cool. My character, Neo, was made because I could relate more to it than being a normal person.
