Welcome to Fursonas/Personas

In general terms, a fursona/persona is a piece of artwork or an idea which can be made to resemble yourself, your state of mind or you may just think the art looks cool.

A fursona is the same as a persona however, you may give it more animalistic features.

E.g.

  • Ears
  • Tails
  • Paws
  • Etc.

These Fursonas can be made into artwork like usual but they can also be used to create a reference sheet for other artists. You can then pay others to draw that fursona that you made and by using a reference sheet, they can create artwork which is more accurate to your own character. They can also be used to make a fursuit of your character. This is explained in more detail on a later page.

You can pay artists to make you a reference sheet for you. Then the reference sheets can get made into a fursuit in the future. (More Information on the Fursuit Page)
