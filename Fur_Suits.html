Welcome to Fursuits

A fursuit is a physical representation of your fursona made into a wearable costume. These can be designed to resemble any species of animal; real or fake. Some people even design their own species, such as the Dutch Angel Dragons (Image 2: Telephone) designed by Theinodog (Ino89777 - Youtube).

There are are loads of different makers around the world designing and making the fursuits you can find on the internet. All you need to do for the comission is give the creator a character sheet (Sonas/Personas), give them any specific information about sizes and any additional features.

People have decided to go to different conventions around the world to wear their fursuits with other people with the same interests.

As previously mentioned in the Sonas/Personas page, fursuits are designed by using a character's reference sheet.
